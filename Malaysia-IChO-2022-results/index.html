    Malaysia IChO 2022 Results

    less than 1 minute read

    The Malaysia IChO 2022 team made our country proud winning 1 Silver and 2 Bronzes.

    Detailed results can be found soon at the IChO 2022 results page.

    Low Zhen Xuen, Brandon

    Silver medal

    Joyton Fu Hung Li

    Bronze medal

    Pua E Rick

    Bronze medal

    Teh Kang Yao

    Team member

    Students interested in the chemistry Olympiad should look out for Kuiz Kimia Kebangsaan (K3M), which is the national selection test in Malaysia for IChO.

    Congratulations to the team, and we wish them all the best!
